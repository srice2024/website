About Us

Welcome to Middleton's Best, a veteran and family-owned transport company dedicated to providing exceptional box truck and sprinter van services for commercial travel across state lines. Founded on the principles of discipline, integrity, and commitment to excellence, our company leverages years of military and logistical expertise to deliver reliable and efficient transportation solutions. At Middleton's Best, we understand the importance of timely and secure delivery. Our team is committed to ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on schedule. Whether it's a single load or recurring shipments, we tailor our services to meet your unique needs, always prioritizing customer satisfaction and trust. Join us on our mission to keep America moving. With Middleton's Best, you're not just a client; you're part of our extended family.
